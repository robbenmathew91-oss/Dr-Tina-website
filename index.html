Welcome to the Salguero Lab

The Salguero Research Group at the University of Georgia (UGA) explores the frontier of inorganic materials and surface chemistry. Our primary scientific focus is on the discovery of novel parent layered compounds, the chemistry of chemical exfoliation into single-layer nanosheets, and the assembly of these 2D structures into functional hybrid networks for energy and electronic applications.

We combine advanced synthetic chemistry (such as high-temperature solid-state reactions, flux synthesis, and hydrothermal methods) with modern characterization tools including X-ray Diffraction (XRD), Scanning and Transmission Electron Microscopy (SEM/TEM), and atomic force microscopy (AFM).

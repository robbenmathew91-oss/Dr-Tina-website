## 3. General Licensing and Re-use Policy

- **University of Georgia Imagery:** Headshots of faculty and students are owned by the University of Georgia. Re-use on the official group website is covered under academic internal use.
- **National Science Foundation (NSF) Slides:** These slides are project outcome summaries submitted to the NSF. Since they are marked as copyrighted in the NSF metadata, the lab retains ownership and they are safe to show on the lab's official portal.
- **Placeholder SVGs:** The `placeholder.svg` avatar is an original, dependency-free vector icon created for this project and is 100% free to use and distribute under open-source licenses.

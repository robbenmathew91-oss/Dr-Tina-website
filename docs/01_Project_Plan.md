# Project Plan: Dr. Tina Salguero Research Group Website

This document outlines the project scope, objectives, target audience, milestones, and implementation timeline for developing the official website for Dr. Tina Salguero's research group at the University of Georgia (UGA).

---

## 1. Project Objectives
- **Professional Representation:** Present the research group's scientific focus, equipment, and capabilities to the academic community, funding agencies, and potential collaborators.
- **Recruitment:** Attract high-caliber graduate, undergraduate, and post-doctoral researchers by highlighting active projects, student achievements, and lab life.
- **Scientific Dissemination:** Maintain an up-to-date repository of publications, active grants, and group news.
- **Modern User Experience:** Provide a fully responsive, accessible, and high-performance website with modern styling and UGA branding compatibility.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Prospective Researchers:** Graduate and undergraduate students interested in joining the lab.
- **Academic Peers & Collaborators:** Scientists at UGA and other institutions looking to learn about the lab's research or collaborate.
- **Funding Agencies:** Representatives from organizations supporting the group's research.
- **General Public:** Individuals interested in materials chemistry and the group's public outreach.

## 4. Deliverables
1. **Source Code:** Standard, modern front-end codebase (HTML/CSS/JS) with reusable components.
2. **Design Assets:** Fully-scalable SVGs, optimized web images, and visual documentation.
3. **Structured Database:** Easy-to-maintain JSON database files representing the lab content.
4. **Documentation:** Complete developer hand-off guides, content checklists, and development logs.

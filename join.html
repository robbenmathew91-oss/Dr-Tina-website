Join the Group

Research opportunities in materials chemistry.

We are always looking for motivated, curious, and hard-working researchers to join our team. Students in the Salguero Group gain hands-on training in solid-state and hydrothermal chemical synthesis, colloidal chemistry, spin coating, and advanced characterization tools (including XRD, SEM, TEM, and AFM).

Graduate Students

Prospective graduate researchers must first gain admission to the Ph.D. or M.S. program in the UGA Department of Chemistry. Once admitted, you are invited to rotate in our lab during the Fall semester.

Undergraduates

We welcome UGA undergraduates for credit (CHEM 3900/4900). We require a commitment of at least 2 consecutive semesters, and approximately 9–12 hours of lab work per week. Ideal for chemistry and engineering majors.

How to Contact Us

If you are interested in joining our lab, please email Dr. Salguero directly at salguero@uga.edu with the subject line "Salguero Lab Inquiry - [Your Name]". Please include the following details:

  • A brief explanation of your research interests and why you want to work in our group.
  • Your current CV / resume (with details of any chemistry courses taken).
  • Contact information for 2 references (academic or research mentors).

We encourage applicants from diverse backgrounds in chemistry, physics, and materials engineering to apply.
